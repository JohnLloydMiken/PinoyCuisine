Adobo Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate the meat with soy sauce, garlic, and peppercorns for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, then fry the meat until lightly browned.
  3. Add the marinade, vinegar, bay leaves, and water to the pan.
  4. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until the meat is tender.
  5. Adjust the flavor with sugar, if desired. Serve with steamed rice.

Indredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
